Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between free and premium plans? +

Our free plan offers basic API access with limited calls and standard support. Premium plans provide increased limits, priority support, advanced features, and dedicated resources to support business-critical applications.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later? +

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with pro-rated charges, while downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my API call limit? +

We'll notify you as you approach your limit. If you exceed it, you'll be automatically charged for overages at our standard rate, or you can upgrade your plan to accommodate your increased usage.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment? +

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with no penalty.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions? +

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team for more information.
